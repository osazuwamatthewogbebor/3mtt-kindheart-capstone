Our Mission

The Digital Curator Approach

In a world of transactional marketplaces, we chose to be curators. Every campaign on KindHeart is presented with an editorial lens—ensuring that the dignity of the storyteller is preserved and the impact of the donor is maximized.

We leverage sophisticated design and transparent technology to bridge the gap between those who need help and those who want to give, creating a seamless cycle of hope.

Our Journey

2021

The Spark

KindHeart was born from a simple observation: stories have the power to change the world, but they need the right stage. We launched with a team of three in a shared garage.

2022

Expanding the Reach

We hit our first ₦10M milestone. The community grew as we partnered with local NGOs to ensure every Naira reached its destination with 100% transparency.

2023

The Digital Curator

Redefined our identity. We moved away from generic lists to a story-first editorial experience, increasing campaign success rates by 45%.
