1. Introduction & Commitment

Kindred ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users. When you use KindHeart, you're trusting us with your information. This Privacy Policy means to help you understand the stories and data you share with us.

We design our systems with your privacy in mind. Transparency isn't just part of our mission to curate hope through collective action; it's a part of our mission to curate hope through collective action.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data

Information provided during registration, name, email address, phone number, physical address, profile information, and communication preferences.

Financial Data

Payment information for processing donations. We use secure third-party payment processors and never store full card numbers on our servers.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and information about how you interact with our platform to ensure security and performance.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Campaign Management: To help you manage your campaigns and connect with supporters
  • Transaction Processing: To securely facilitate and move of funds from donors to campaigns
  • Platform Evolution: To improve our editorial experience and improve the editorial platform

4. Data Sharing

Service Providers

We work with trusted partners to help us host your data, process payments, and provide support. These partners comply with strict data protection standards.

Legal Compliance

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our community.
