Frequently Asked Questions

The most common things people ask us.

How do I start a campaign? expand_more
To start a campaign, simply click the "Start Funding" button in the navigation. You'll need to verify your identity, tell your story with clear goals, and upload high-quality images to inspire trust in your donors.
Is my donation secure? expand_more
Yes. We use industry-standard bank-level encryption and partner with global payment leaders like Stripe to ensure your financial data is never stored on our servers and every transaction is protected.
How do I get my funds? expand_more
Once your campaign reaches its goal or a scheduled payout date, funds are transferred directly to your linked bank account. This typically takes 3-5 business days depending on your local banking regulations.
Are there platform fees? expand_more
KindHeart operates on a donor-tip model. We don't take a percentage of the funds you raise for individual causes, though standard credit card processing fees from our payment providers still apply.
