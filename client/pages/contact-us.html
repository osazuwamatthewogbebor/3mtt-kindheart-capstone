Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a campaign?

To start a campaign, create an account, fill in your campaign details, and submit for review. Our team will review your campaign within 24-48 hours.

Is my donation secure?

Yes! All donations are processed using industry-leading SSL encryption and PCI compliance standards to ensure your payment information is secure.

How do I get my funds?

Once your campaign reaches its funding goal, funds are transferred to your verified bank account within 5-7 business days. We handle all payment processing.

Are there platform fees?

KindHeart charges a 5% platform fee on successful campaigns. Payment processors also charge standard transaction fees (typically 2-3% per donation).
