How KindHeart Works

Create a campaign, share it, and start raising support in a few simple steps.

1

Create an account

Register as a user so you can create campaigns, manage your profile, and track your activity in one place.

2

Start a campaign

Add your title, category, goal, description, and image. The campaign page gives supporters the context they need to help.

3

Share and raise funds

Share your campaign link with your network and monitor donations as support comes in.
